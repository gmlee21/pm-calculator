Project Management Man-Effort Calculator

Remarks: Using the proportion of non-recurring engineering (NRE) manpower cost vis-a-vis the total project manpower cost (i.e. sum of NRE and project management costs, less trial support manpower cost) as yardstick:

Tier 1 - Largely systems integration (NRE cost is less than or equal to 25% of total project manpower cost)

Tier 2 - Minor developement with systems integration (NRE cost is more than 25% and less than or equal to 50% of total project manpower cost)

Tier 3 - Significant developement with systems integration (NRE cost is more than 50% and less than or equal to 75% of total project manpower cost)

Tier 4 - Full scale development (2 - Minor developement with systems integration (NRE cost is more than 75% of total project manpower cost)
